About us

    1.Who we are

A programming language is a way for programmers (developers) to communicate with computers. Programming languages consist of a set of rules that allows string values to be converted into various ways of generating machine code, or, in the case of visual programming languages, graphical elements. rogramming languages are described in terms of their syntax (form) and semantics (meaning), usually defined by a formal language. Languages usually provide features such as a type system, variables and mechanisms for error handling. An implementation of a programming language in the form of a compiler or interpreter allows programs to be executed, either directly or by producing an executable.

    2. Mission

Computer Programming aims to train individuals who are educated with theoretical and practical knowledge on course programs, basic programming logic, database, internet programming, network systems, web design, computer hardware that are specified in accordance with current technologies. The department of Computer Programming focuses on collecting information about the problems of business and social life and for transactions directed to processing this collected information. Our department mainly targets to train qualified graduates who can produce solutions for potential problems and develop algorithms and programs. Students of OSTIM Technical University-Vocational School of Computer Programming will be taught English preparation depending on their requests. The medium of instruction of the program is Turkish.

    3. Vision

The mission of this program is to train computer technicians who have the knowledge and skills required by businesses about rapidly increasing computer technologies in our country and in the world, who can adapt to changing technology and renew themselves, know and use computer hardware and software well.Computer Programming has adopted the vision of training qualified technical staff who can meet the needs of the society in accordance with international standards in its field.
