About


Department of Applied Science is a multidisciplinary department. The core courses being offered are Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English and Principles of Engineering Economics and Management. Department has a team of dedicated faculty members who are actively engaged in imparting quality teaching in basic sciences and humanities to the engineering students. It is an endeavor of the department to provide students such an ambience that they have holistic growth leading to excellence in their professional life as well as social life. Apart from the undergraduate courses, the department is also pursuing research activities in the above-mentioned disciplines and is currently having research scholars in various subjects. Department welcomes committed minds to join hands to build a strong, dynamic and wholesome work place.
